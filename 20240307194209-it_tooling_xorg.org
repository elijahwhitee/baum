#+title: it/tooling/xorg

* Configuring screen dpi
Changing the dpi with xrandr
#+begin_src bash
xrandr --dpi 144 # replace with any number, may affect the appearance of icons
#+end_src

 Querying the current dpi
 #+begin_src bash
xdpyinfo | grep dots
 #+end_src
